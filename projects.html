My Projects

A selection of projects that highlight my skills in UX design, planning, and development.

Tourister – Travel Guide App (UX + Feature Planning)

Designed and planned features for Tourister, a smart travel guide app that simplifies trip planning. Developed user flow, organized destination search, integrated historical and seasonal insights, and included guide contact tools. Focused on creating a user-friendly experience that empowers travelers with all-in-one destination info.

TrackUp – Smart Educational Roadmap

TrackUp is a modern, AI-powered platform that helps students organize their academic journey and shape their career path. With a clean and futuristic design, TrackUp allows users to register, manage their academic details, and access personalized study and career roadmaps based on their branch or department. It suggests the right skills, courses, and technologies to focus on—making learning structured and goal-oriented.
